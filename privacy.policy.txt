Privacy Policy

Just Enough Light is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Usage
User Preferences: The extension stores your settings (e.g., mask strength and radius) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data is used solely to provide a personalized and consistent experience when using the extension.

Data Collection
No Data Collection: The extension does not collect or transmit any data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

Permissions
Storage: This permission is used only to save and retrieve user preferences.
Host Permissions: The extension requires access to all websites (<all_urls>) to apply the mask overlay and enhance the browsing experience. However, it does not read or process any content on these websites.

Third-Party Services
No Third-Party Integration: This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.
